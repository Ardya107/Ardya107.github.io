Poster Design - Gujuyo

This poster design was created as part of a high school entrepreneurship project for a brand named Gujuyo, short for Guava Juice Yoghurt. The poster showcases the essence of the product through vibrant visuals, a clean layout, and carefully chosen typography.

The design features a bright and inviting color palette dominated by pink and white, reflecting the freshness and sweetness of guava juice combined with the creamy texture of yogurt. A bottle of Gujuyo takes center stage in the poster, surrounded by playful elements to enhance its appeal to the target audience.

This project highlights my ability to transform branding concepts into impactful visuals that effectively communicate a message while captivating the audience.
